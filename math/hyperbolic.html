Definitions of hyperbolic functions

sinhx=ex−e−x2
coshx=ex+e−x2
tanhx=ex−e−xex+e−x=sinhxcoshx
cschx=2ex−e−x=1sinhx
sechx=2ex+e−x=1coshx
cothx=ex+e−xex−e−x=coshxsinhx
